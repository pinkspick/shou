LA MAISON

A House Built on Light

There is an old belief that a diamond holds a fragment of a fallen star. At Lumière, we did not wish to wait for the heavens, nor to wound the earth in search of them. So we learned to grow stars of our own.

In our ateliers, carbon is gathered into a chamber of pure energy. Over weeks, it remembers what it has always longed to become. Layer by layer, a crystal awakens — identical in fire, in hardness, in soul, to any drawn from a mine, yet born without a single scar upon the land.

Each creation is a chapter. Each chapter, a poem of patience, science, and the human hand. This is our heritage — written not in centuries of extraction, but in a promise to the century ahead.

THE SCIENCE OF LIGHT

Indistinguishable. Except in Its Origin.

A Lumière diamond is not an imitation. It is a diamond — chemically, physically, optically identical to those formed over a billion years. The only difference is the gentleness of its making.

  • 10 on the Mohs scale — the hardest material on earth, mined or grown.
  • 57 facets, hand-polished to release maximum fire and scintillation.
  • 100% renewable energy across every cultivation hall.
  • D–F colour, VVS clarity — only the rarest grades bear our name.

END TO END

From a Seed of Carbon to Your Hand

Every Lumière diamond travels a transparent path. We invite you to follow it — there are no shadows in our supply chain.

01

The Seed

A sliver of diamond — the seed — is placed in a reactor. Renewable energy ignites a plasma of carbon-rich gas. Crystal growth begins, atom by atom.

02

The Cultivation

Over six to ten weeks, the diamond grows in a controlled cosmos. No earth is moved, no river dredged, no community displaced.

03

The Reading

Each rough is mapped by master cutters. Like reading the grain of rare wood, they find the angle that will sing with the most fire.

04

The Cut

Precision lasers and human eyes carve 57 facets. Polished against diamond dust until each face is a flawless mirror.

05

The Setting

Goldsmiths set the stone in 100% recycled gold or platinum, by hand, in our atelier. Every prong a small act of devotion.

06

The Certificate

Your diamond arrives with its origin passport — grade, carbon footprint, and the very hall in which it was grown. Traceable to the atom.
