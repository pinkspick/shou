LA MAISON

A House Built on Light

There is an old belief that a diamond holds a fragment of a fallen star. At Lumière, we did not wish to wait for the heavens, nor to wound the earth in search of them. So we learned to grow stars of our own.

In our ateliers, carbon is gathered into a chamber of pure energy. Over weeks, it remembers what it has always longed to become — a crystal identical in fire, in hardness, in soul, to any drawn from a mine, yet born without a single scar upon the land.

Each creation is a chapter. Each chapter, a poem of patience, science, and the human hand.

I

The Garden of Carbon

Where light and gas become the first whisper of a crystal — powered entirely by renewable energy.

II

The Cutter's Vow

Master lapidaries read each rough by hand, releasing 57 facets of fire from a single point of brilliance.

III

The Setting of a Story

Goldsmiths cradle every stone in recycled gold and platinum — a clean conscience in every prong.

THE SCIENCE OF LIGHT

Indistinguishable. Except in Its Origin.

A Lumière diamond is not an imitation. It is a diamond — chemically, physically, optically identical to those formed over a billion years. The only difference is the gentleness of its making.

  • 10 on the Mohs scale — the hardest material on earth, mined or grown.
  • 57 facets, hand-polished to release maximum fire and scintillation.
  • 100% renewable energy across every cultivation hall.
  • D–F colour, VVS clarity — only the rarest grades bear our name.

END TO END

From a Seed of Carbon to Your Hand

Every Lumière diamond travels a transparent path. There are no shadows in our supply chain.

01

The Seed

A sliver of diamond is placed in a reactor. Renewable energy ignites a plasma of carbon-rich gas. Growth begins, atom by atom.

02

The Cultivation

Over six to ten weeks, the diamond grows in a controlled cosmos. No earth moved, no river dredged.

03

The Reading

Each rough is mapped by master cutters, who find the angle that will sing with the most fire.

04

The Cut

Precision lasers and human eyes carve 57 facets, polished against diamond dust to a flawless mirror.

05

The Setting

Goldsmiths set the stone in recycled gold or platinum, by hand, in our atelier.

06

The Certificate

Your diamond arrives with its origin passport — traceable to the atom.
